Vocabulary Data Files

The application's vocabulary is sourced from a collection of JSON files located in the /data directory. This modular approach makes it easy to manage and expand the word lists.

Available Files:

  • adjectives.json - Adjectives
  • adverbs.json - Adverbs
  • basics.json - Basic terms (days, months, questions, etc.)
  • nouns.json - Nouns with articles and plurals
  • numbers.json - Numbers from 0 to 20
  • prepositions.json - Prepositions with associated cases
  • pronouns.json - Personal and possessive pronouns
  • sentences.json - Common sentences and phrases
  • verbs.json - Verbs
